Google Sheets Setup

Shared real-time database for Galicia & Plantas

Both partners access the same Google Sheet in real time. No server, no cost.
1

Create a Google Sheet

Go to sheets.new, name it GP Lending Database, share with your partner as Editor.

2

Create 3 tabs with exact headers

Borrowers:  id | name | contact | address | date_added
Loans:      id | borrower_id | borrower_name | principal | ontime_rate | overdue_rate | start_date | due_date | status | notes
Payments:   id | loan_id | borrower_name | date | amount | interest_settled | unpaid_interest_cf | principal_reduced | principal_balance | notes
3
